Paragraph Tag:

Used to define a paragraph of text. It ignores extra spaces, newlines, and tabs. . o .. o . o o.o ...oo __[]__ __|_o_o_o\__ \""""""""""/ \. .. . / ^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^

tag **won't** be displayed correctly.

Preformatted Text Tag:

Preserves all spaces, newlines, and tabs exactly as written.

    .  o ..                  
    o . o o.o                
         ...oo               
           __[]__            
        __|_o_o_o\__         
        \""""""""""/         
         \. ..  . /          
    ^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^
