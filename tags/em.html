Em Tag

The < i > and <em > tags both make text italic, but they have different meanings and uses:

  1. < b > (italic)
    • Used for Styling only (without adding meaning).
    • Commonly used for things like technical terms, foreign words, or citations.
    • Extample: The word résumé comes from French . So here resume is italic for style but does not carry extra emphasis.
  2. < em > (emphasis)
    • Used to add emphasis to the text.
    • Screen readers stress the word differently, helping visually impaired users.
    • Example: You must submit the form before 5 PM.This tells the reader that must is important and should be stressed.
